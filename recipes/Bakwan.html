Deskription

Bakwan Goreng, also known in other regions as bala-bala, is one of the most popular types of gorengan (assorted Indonesian fritters). It is essentially a savory vegetable fritter, made from a simple flour batter packed with shredded vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and bean sprouts. The mixture is then deep-fried until it's golden brown and crispy on the outside, while remaining soft and perfectly cooked on the inside.

Ingredients

  • The vegetables
    • 150g cabbage, thinly sliced
    • 2 medium-sized carrots, coarsely grated
    • 100g bean sprotus
    • 2 stalks scallions, thinly sliced
    • Opstional additions : Sweets corn kernels, or small peeled shrimp to make Bakwan Udang
  • The Batter
    • 200g All-pupose flour
    • 50g rice flour
    • 1 Egg
    • 250-300ml ice-cold water
  • The seasoning paste
    • 4 clover garlic
    • 3 shallots
    • 1 tsp whole peppercorns or 1/2tsp ground white pepper
    • tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp granulated siugar
  • Cooking oil
  1. Prepare the vegetables
  2. Make the seasoning paste
  3. Combine the batter
  4. Add water
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Fry the bakwan
  7. Drain and serve
