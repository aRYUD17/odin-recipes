Description

Indomie Goreng has transcended its status as a mere instant noodle to become a global culinary phenomenon and a beloved comfort food for millions. Originating from Indonesia, this iconic dish is the instant adaptation of the traditional Indonesian stir-fried noodle dish, mie goreng.
